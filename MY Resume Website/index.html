My name is Yusuf ODUKOYA. I am an indigene of Ogun, south-west Nigeria. I see myself as a dogged hustler with a huge enthusiasm for ICT. I am an ardent lover of graphic design as an art. Writing code and designing graphic assets bring me a pure feeling of joy! I started my graphic design career when I was just 14 - Over a decade ago. However, I did not start rendering professional creative design services until after my first year as an undergraduate of Zoology at the University of Lagos. I helped myself financially through school with this skill. As time went on, I started to feel like something was missing! This feeling kept haunting me until I learned Frontend Web Design. Since 2007, I have learned various concepts in both creative design and web development, some of which have become a part of my philosophy as a designer.

I earned my Bachelors degree (Zoology, Parasitology Option) in 2016 at the University of Lagos.

In July 2017, I was posted to Niger State, where I served as a Computer Studies teacher in the small village of Ibeto, Magama Local Government for a year.

Personal Achievements

Asides earning a degree in Zoology, I have built several web based products, and I maintain a portfolio of cool graphic designs. I have worked with entrepreneurs from the UK and East Africa. I have also built some websites and designed logos for several small and medium-scale businesses. As a result, their respective businesses have witnessed a significant growth. I consider that an achievement.

I am currently looking forward to working in a company that would provide me with the opportunity to express my creativity.
